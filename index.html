Yan-Ting Chen is a senior undergraduate student in Economics at National Taiwan University, with an expected graduation in December 2026. He currently serves as a Research Assistant at the TASSEL Lab under Prof. Joseph Dao-Yi Wang, developing computational methods to quantify linguistic structure emergence in multi-agent human experiments. Previously, he conducted undergraduate research at the iDSSP Lab on demographic fairness in recommendation systems, achieving 6–20× fairness improvements with less than 1% accuracy trade-off. His industry experience includes a Fintech × Generative AI Research Internship at Cathay United Bank, a Technology Consulting Internship at Ernst & Young, and a Software Engineering Internship at Flexsoft.
